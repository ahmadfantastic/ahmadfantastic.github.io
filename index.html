Hi! I am a PhD candidate of Computing and Informations Science at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), advised by Daqing Hou.

Research

I'm a computing education researcher focused on project-based learning (PjBL), software engineering, and applied large language models (LLMs). My work builds scalable PjBL modules and LLM-based teaching assistants to support student learning and faculty adoption.

  • Project-based learning in computing education (design, assessment, faculty adoption)
  • LLM assistants: Propmpt engineering, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), fine-tuning, and LLM agents for education
  • High-performance computing for large-scale experiments and simulations
